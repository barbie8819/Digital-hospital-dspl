Chronic Kidney Disease(CKD)

Symptoms: Fatigue Swelling (edema) High blood pressure Changes in urination patterns Blood or protein in the urine • Causes: • Diabetes Hypertension Glomerulonephritis Lifestyle factors Cures/Treatment: Medications Dietary changes Dialysis (if necessary) Kidney transplantation (in advanced stages)

Know more...

Kidney Stones

Symptoms: Severe pain in back, side, or abdomen Blood in urine Frequent urination Nausea and vomiting •Causes: Mineral and salt buildup Dehydration Diet high in oxalate-rich foods Cures/Treatment: Increased fluid intake Medication Shock wave lithotripsy Ureteroscopy Surgery (for larger stones).

Know more...

Polycystic Kidney Disease

Normal kidney Polycystic kidney Symptoms: Abdominal pain High blood pressure Kidney stones Frequent urination Urinary tract infections Causes: • Genetic mutation (usually inherited) • Cures/Treatment: Blood pressure management Pain relief Addressing complications • Kidney transplantation (in severe cases)

Know more...

Alport Syndrome

Symptoms: Hematuria (blood in urine) Hearing loss Eye abnormalities Causes: Genetic mutation in collagen genes Cures/Treatment: Management of symptoms Kidney transplantation (in severe cases)

Know more...

Neprotic Syndrome

Swelling (edema) Proteinuria (excessive protein in urine) High cholesterol levels • Causes: Primary (idiopathic) Secondary (due to other medical conditions) Cures/Treatment: Medications (e.g., steroids) Diuretics Dietary changes Treating underlying causes
